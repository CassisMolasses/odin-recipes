Binary Burger Recipe

This gourmet burger combines the savory richness of the beef patty with the sweetness of caramelized onions, the kick of sriracha, and the creaminess of Gruyère cheese. It's a deliciously balanced binary experience for your taste buds!

    INGREDIENTS
  • For the Burger Patties:
    • 1 pound (450g) high-quality ground beef (80% lean, 20% fat)
    • 1/2 cup finely minced caramelized onions
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
    • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • For the Binary Sauce:
    • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
    • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
    • 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce
    • 1 tablespoon finely chopped chives
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For Assembly:
    • Brioche burger buns, toasted
    • Gruyère or Swiss cheese slices
    • Crispy bacon strips
    • Arugula or baby spinach leaves
    • Sliced ripe tomatoes
  1. Prepare the Binary Sauce:
    • In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, sriracha sauce, chopped chives, salt, and pepper. Set aside.
  2. Make the Burger Patties:
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine ground beef, minced caramelized onions, minced garlic, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper.
    • Gently mix the ingredients until well combined, taking care not to overwork the meat.
    • Divide the mixture into equal portions and shape them into burger patties.
  3. Grill or Cook the Patties:
    • Preheat the grill or a skillet over medium-high heat.
    • Cook the burger patties to your desired doneness, approximately 4-5 minutes per side for medium.
    • In the last minute of cooking, place a slice of Gruyère or Swiss cheese on each patty and cover to melt.
  4. Assemble the Binary Burger:
    • Spread a generous amount of the Binary Sauce on the toasted brioche buns.
    • Place the cheese-topped burger patties on the bottom bun.
    • Add crispy bacon strips on top of the patties.
    • Layer with arugula or baby spinach leaves and sliced ripe tomatoes.
    • Top with the other half of the brioche bun.
  5. Serve and Enjoy:
    • Serve "The Binary Burger" with your favorite side dishes and enjoy the unique blend of flavors.
