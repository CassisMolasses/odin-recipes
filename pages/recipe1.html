DEV-iled Eggs Recipe

    INGREDIENTS
  • 6 hard-boiled eggs, peeled and halved
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon white wine vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon hot sauce (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Paprika and chives for garnish
    INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Prepare the Eggs:
    • Hard-boil the eggs by placing them in a saucepan, covering them with water, and boiling for 10 minutes.
    • Once cooked, cool the eggs quickly by placing them in an ice water bath.
    • Peel the eggs and cut them in half lengthwise.
  2. Devilish Filling:
    • Carefully remove the yolks from the egg halves and place them in a bowl.
    • Mash the yolks with a fork until they are crumbly.
    • Add mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, white wine vinegar, hot sauce, salt, and pepper to the yolks.
    • Mix until the filling is smooth and well combined.
  3. DEV-ilish Assembly:
    • Spoon or pipe the devilish filling back into the egg white halves, creating a rounded top.
    • For a tech twist, you can use a piping bag with a star tip to pipe the filling for a decorative touch.
  4. Garnish:
    • Sprinkle each DEV-iled egg with a pinch of paprika for a pop of color and flavor.
    • Finely chop chives and sprinkle them on top for a fresh, green garnish.
  5. Serve:
    • Arrange the DEV-iled eggs on a platter and serve chilled.
    • These playful and tech-inspired eggs are a delightful appetizer for parties or gatherings.
