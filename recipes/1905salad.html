1905 Salad

Description

This recipe comes from The Columbia Gem of Spanish Restaurants. The original location opened in 1905 in Ibor City, Florida. and my family has been dining there for generations. This is a must visit destination if you are visiting Florida!

This recipe is so easy. I would have to describe it as the a perfectly savory salad that is suprisingly very filling. Follow along with my recipe below to have a taste of my favorite restaurant in the Sunshine State?

Ingredients

Dressing

  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • juice of half a lemon
  • 2 tbsp worchestershire sauce

Salad

  • iceberg lettuce
  • tomato
  • manzanilla olives
  • diced ham
  • parmasaen cheese
  • swiss cheese
