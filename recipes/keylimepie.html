Key Lime Pie

Description

Key lime pie is an easy to make dessert with very few ingredients, but oh so delicious!

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 pk Graham Crackers
  • 2 1/2 Tbsp Sugar

Filling

  • 3 Large Egg Yokes
  • 1 Tbsp Lime Zest
  • 1/2 Cup Strained Lime Juice
  • 14 oz can Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 1/4 Cup Sour Cream

Topping

  • 3/4 Cup Heavy Cream
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp Sugar

Directions
