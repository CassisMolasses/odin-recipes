Key Lime Pie

Description

Key lime pie is an easy to make dessert with very few ingredients, but oh so delicious!

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 pk Graham Crackers
  • 2 1/2 Tbsp Sugar

Filling

  • 3 Large Egg Yokes
  • 1 Tbsp Lime Zest
  • 1/2 Cup Strained Lime Juice
  • 14 oz can Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 1/4 Cup Sour Cream

Topping

  • 3/4 Cup Heavy Cream
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp Sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Crush Graham crackers and combine with sugar and melted butter.
  3. Pour this mixture in a pie pan and press smooth using your hands or a spoon.
  4. Bake until golden brown in preheted oven approximately 11-13 minutes.
  5. Remove crust from oven and set aside to cool. Meanwhile you will prepare the filling.
  6. Lower the oven temperature to 325 degrees.
  7. Whisk together egg yokes and lime zest.
  8. Stir in sweetened condensed milk, lime juice and, sour cream and mix until blended.
  9. Set mixture aside to rest and thicken for 15 minutes.
  10. Pour the filling into the cooled crust and bake for 15-17 minutes.
  11. Once baked, allow pie to rest and cool for 45 minutes before placeing it in the fridge for atleast 4 hours.
  12. Prepare whipped topping by blending heavy whipping cream and sugar til stiff.
  13. Pipe on whipped topping and serve!
